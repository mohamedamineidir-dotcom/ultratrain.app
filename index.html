Everything You Need to Reach the Finish Line

Built by runners, for runners. Every feature designed around the unique demands of ultra trail running.

📋

Personalized Training Plans

Periodized plans built from your A-race backwards. Base, build, peak, and taper phases calibrated to your fitness level, with automatic B/C race integration.

🍌

Race Nutrition Strategy

Calorie, hydration, and electrolyte plans based on your weight and race profile. Gut training reminders during long runs to practice race-day fueling.

📍

GPS Run Tracking

Live pace, distance, elevation, and heart rate with auto-pause detection. Post-run splits, elevation profiles, and HR zone analysis.

⏱

Finish Time Prediction

Effort-based estimates using your training data and race elevation profile. Three scenarios — optimistic, expected, conservative — with per-checkpoint splits.

📈

Fitness & Training Load

Track weekly volume, elevation, and time. Monitor fitness, fatigue, and form trends with acute-to-chronic ratio alerts to prevent overtraining.

⚡

Apple Watch & Widgets

Standalone watch tracking with GPS and heart rate. Home screen widgets for next session, race countdown, weekly progress, and live activity during runs.

Get Started in Minutes

From download to your first training plan in under 5 minutes.

1

Set Up Your Profile

Enter your experience level, running history, and physical data. Connect HealthKit to import your training history automatically.

2

Add Your Target Race

Enter your A-race details — distance, elevation, date, and goal. Add B/C races as training milestones if you have them.

3

Get Your Plan

UltraTrain generates a periodized training plan with integrated nutrition strategy. Adjust sessions as needed — the plan adapts to you.

4

Train & Track

Track runs with GPS, monitor your fitness trends, and watch your predicted finish time improve as you get stronger.
